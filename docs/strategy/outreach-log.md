# Outreach log

Every deliberate push of Elsewhere out into a community or channel, so we gather feedback and learn instead of blasting and forgetting. One entry per outreach, newest first. Pair the qualitative feedback here with the hard numbers from `/teaser-stats` (calls · unique · repeat · signups · conversion% · avg duration) for the window after each send.

**How to use:** log the exact copy sent, where and when, the intent, and then come back and fill in results + learnings. Discipline (from [go-to-market.md](go-to-market.md)): leak, don't spray. These are seed pushes into high-fit micro-communities, not a marketing machine. Watch for unprompted sharing — that's the Stage-0 signal, not raw reach.
